About Me

I was born and raised in Vernon, BC. I attended Okanagan Landing Elementary School and Fulton Secondary High School. Growing up, I played a numerous amount of sports. including hockey, soccer, basketball, volleyball, skiing, and dance. Our soccer team won the provincial championship in .... Playing several team sports taught me to work with others and developed my communication skills. I will never forget the fun I had during these sports.

Education

Growing up I was always a high achiever. Throughout elementary it was a goal of mine to get straight A's. Math always came easy to me. My parents noticed my math skills at a young age contrary to my writing. In grade 12 there was a calculus course offered and it was this class that pursued me to major in math in University. For first year sciences I went to Okanagan College in Vernon and transfered to the University of British Columbia for my second and following years.

Work Experience

After completing a Math degree my goal was to become a Chartered Professional Accountant. I spent 6 months at BDO. Most of this was preparing tax returns. Although becoming an accountant would have been a solid decision, there was something about it that didn't fuel my passion. Following this, I worked at Nadine's Fine Art and Frames for several months and after moved to Salmon Arm to work as a bookkeeper. I worked at Salmon Arm Bookkeeping and Business Services for two years.
